VISTADOME COACH

Vistadome is a state-of-the-art coach made by Indian Railways, designed to provide passengers with travel comfort as well as an enhanced viewing experience of their surroundings.













Speciality


Great Livery!
(hover)

Livery

The LHB new version of Vistadome is in the blue and white livery. The old ICF vistadome coach has blue and grey livery. The Mhow Omkareshwar MG vistadome has jungle livery and the Kalka Shimla NG vistadome has purple livery

Great Interior!
(hover)

Interior

Spacious, clean and Comfortable interior with excellent features. Wide Windows and windows on ceiling. AC coaches in BG and non-AC coaches in MG and NG

Great View!
(hover)

View

Wide windows, Glass Roof Top and rotatable seats enables to get a beautiful view of the ghats. At the end of the coach, a lounge like space is provided width a large window for backside view (The view is blocked by the locomotive during the return trip)

Great Features!
(hover)

Features

Wide windows, Roatatble seat, Glass rooftop, Push-back chairs, Wi-Fi, CCTV Surviellence, Charging Sockets, Snack Table, Automatic sliding doors, LED Display, Automatic fire alarm, Multi-tiered Steel Luggage Shelves Bio toilets, tourist friendly louge, etc.

Great Food!
(hover)

Food

The pantry has basic snacks, baked goodies, Maggi, soft drinks, and beverages like tea and coffee. These are available on a request and paid basis. The food is stocked up at all the main junctions. If you want a lunch or dinner, you need to let the staff know in advance.

MG and NG
(hover)

MG and NG coaches

The Meter and narrow gauge coaches are mostly non-AC with less facilities. Wi-fi, Automatic Doors, rotatable seats, etc are the facilities which are not there. Food is not provided.
